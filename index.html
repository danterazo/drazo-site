Welcome to Dante's Page

I'm a junior at Indiana University studying Computer Science with focuses on artificial intelligence and data science. I'm working towards minors in linguistics and computational linguistics as well. I work at IU's School of Informatics, Computing and Engineering and Kelley School of Business as a peer advisor and computer technician respectively. I have been programming for almost 11 years with experience in Java, Python, and R, to name a few languages. I hope to work with machine learning in the future; specifically data science, AI, or natural language processing.

Jungle: Java-Based Marketplace

An Amazon-esque marketplace written in Java by a team of three. Jungle lets you register as a buyer or seller, list items for sale, buy items, receive shipping notifications, manage your account, and more. It uses CSV files to keep an offline database of accounts and inventory.

Final project for CSCI-C 212 "Software Systems".

Porto Seguro Safe Driver Prediction

Used data provided by Brazilian auto insurance company Porto Seguro to predict the probability of a driver initiating an insurance claim in the next year. Used R for data preprocessing and Python to build the model.

Final project for CSCI-B 351 "Data Mining and Analysis". View the report here.

Cryptocurrency Analysis AI

Developed an AI in a team of two that predicts when to buy and sell popular cryptocurrencies. I used R for data preprocessing, Python for creating a framework, and the TensorFlow library for training a model.

Final project for CSCI-B 351 "Intro to Artificial Intelligence". View the report here.
